Returning Students

Continuing students of PDBHS

For students who are currently enrolled and continuing their education at Paso De Blas National High School.

Requirements:

  • Student ID number
  • Previous semester grades
  • Clearance from all departments
  • Updated contact information

New Students

First-time in PDBHS

For students who will be enrolling in Paso De Blas National High School for the first time.

Requirements:

  • High school diploma (original and photocopy)
  • Birth certificate (PSA copy)
  • Good moral certificate
  • 2x2 ID photos (4 copies)
  • Medical certificate

Transferees

From other institutions

For students transferring from other schools to Paso De Blas National High School.

Requirements:

  • Transcript of records (original)
  • Honorable dismissal
  • Course description/syllabus
  • 2x2 ID photos (4 copies)
  • Medical certificate
