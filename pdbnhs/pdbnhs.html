Historical Background

Location of the Proposed High School

• The proposed four storey school building with 24 classrooms, intended for the establishment of the Senior high school, is situated at Maysan Road, Paso de Blas, Valenzuela City.

History of the Establishment of School/Initial Undertakings

• The Local School Board of Valenzuela City recognizes the need of Public Senior High School for grade 11 and 12 students of Paso de Blas and to accommodate as well the increasing number of enrollees.

• The Sanguniang Baranggay ng Paso de Blas after a thorough study and upon evaluation and assessment from the Division of City Schools, Valenzuela, it was found out that Barangay Paso de Blas needs the establishment of Maysan National High School-Paso de Blas Senior High School Annex that will serve not only the residents of Barangay Paso de Blas but also students from the neighboring barangays.

• On January 2, 2015 the said Barangay Council has created a Resolution No. Series 2015. A Resolution confirming the establishment of Maysan National High School Paso de Blas Senior High School Annex.

• The establishment of the said school is done through the initiative of the Sanguniang Barangay of Paso de Blas headed by Barangay Captain, Fernando DS Esteban, coupled with the cooperation/support given by the officers of the Parents Teachers Association, as well as the residents of the said barangay. It has been their long dream to have senior high school within the community for their children not to be travelling from a distant place just to finish high school.

Our Mission, Vision, and Core Values

Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential as productive, creative, and responsible members of society.

  • Foster academic excellence through innovative teaching
  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Promote values of integrity, respect, and social responsibility
  • Prepare students for lifelong learning and global citizenship

Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for developing well-rounded individuals who make meaningful contributions to their communities and the world.
